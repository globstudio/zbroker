zpipes(1)
=========

NAME
----
zpipes - elastic pipes proof of concept

SYNOPSIS
--------
----
zpipes servicename
----

DESCRIPTION
-----------
Starts the zpipes service with a given name. To talk to the zpipes service you 
open a ZeroMQ DEALER socket and send commands in the form of multipart messages,
then read replies. Supports two commands:

* PUT | pipename | opaque-data
* GET | pipename | size

Where size is an ascii integer, or empty/zero meaning "as much data as available".
